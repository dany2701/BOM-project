HOW TO PLAY
// BRIEFING
YOUR MISSION,
COMMANDER

You control the Warzone Arcade — a flywheel-powered launcher aimed at the target board. Each mission gives you a specific number of missiles and supplies to deliver in 30 seconds. Hit the sensors, rack up points, complete your objective. Simple. Brutal. Glorious.

01
🎯
AIM YOUR LAUNCHER

Use the joystick to rotate the flywheel shooter left and right. Line up your shot before firing.

02
⚡
FIRE THE BALL

Launch the ball using the flywheel mechanism. Hit the piezoelectric sensor targets on the board to score a point.

03
🔄
LIFT & SORT

The ball travels up the Archimedes screw lift (~30cm), then rolls down a slide into the automatic ball sorter.

04
✅
COMPLETE THE MISSION

Hit enough missiles (black balls) and supplies (white balls) before the 30-second timer runs out!

// BALL TYPES
MISSILE
Black ball — delivers a strike on enemy territory. High risk, high reward.
SUPPLY
White ball — sends support to your allies. Keep them in the fight.
