YOUR MISSION,
COMMANDER

Do you have what it in you to aid us in winning The War of all?
Brave enough to fight against the terrors of the enemy?
Strong enough to aid the weak?

Then You ARE part of THE Republic's Army. You will be given a mission to either supply our allies or rain terror on the frontlines.
The Republic COUNTS ON YOU 🫵🏻

01
🎯
AIM YOUR LAUNCHER

Use the joystick to rotate the flywheel shooter left and right. Line up your shot before firing.

02
⚡
FIRE THE BALL

Launch the ball using the flywheel mechanism. Hit the piezoelectric sensor targets on the board to score a point.

03
✅
COMPLETE THE MISSION

Hit enough missiles (black balls) and supplies (white balls) before the timer runs out!
